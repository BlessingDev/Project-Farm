Rice
Rice is the seed of the grass species Oryza sativa(Asial rice) or Oryza glaberrima(African rice). As a cereal grain, it is the most widely consumed staple food for a large part of the world's human population, especially in Asia. it is the agricultural commodity with the third-highest worldwide production, after sugarcane and maize, according to 2012 FAOSTAT data. Rice, a monocost, is normally grown as an annual plant, although in tropical areas it can survive as a perennial and can produce a ratoon crop for up to 30 years.
